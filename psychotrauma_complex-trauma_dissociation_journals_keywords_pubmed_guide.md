# Psychological Trauma / Complex Trauma / Dissociation  
## Journal List + Focus + Q1/Q2 + Common Keywords + PubMed Search Templates

This file is an English-only working guide for tracking journals relevant to **psychological trauma**, **complex trauma / CPTSD**, and **dissociation**.

## Notes
- **Q1/Q2** below reflects **publicly visible best quartile information** commonly reported in recent public journal-metrics pages (mainly 2024 data, sometimes shown as best quartile rather than a single category-specific rank).
- Quartiles can vary by **database** and **subject category**.
- The **PubMed search template** is designed for quick journal-specific scanning, not for a full systematic review strategy.
- Many trauma papers also appear in broader psychiatry, clinical psychology, developmental psychopathology, and violence journals.

## Recommended workflow
1. Start with the **core trauma journals**.
2. Add **child maltreatment / interpersonal violence journals** for developmental trauma and chronic interpersonal trauma.
3. Add **review journals** when you want fast conceptual orientation.
4. For PubMed, replace or expand the Title/Abstract terms depending on your topic:
   - `complex PTSD`
   - `CPTSD`
   - `dissociation`
   - `dissociative disorder`
   - `depersonalization`
   - `derealization`
   - `DID`
   - `childhood trauma`
   - `maltreatment`
   - `adverse childhood experiences`
   - `peritraumatic dissociation`

## Generic PubMed master template

```text
(
  "Psychological Trauma"[Mesh]
  OR "Trauma and Stressor Related Disorders"[Mesh]
  OR "Stress Disorders, Post-Traumatic"[Mesh]
  OR "Dissociative Disorders"[Mesh]
  OR trauma*[Title/Abstract]
  OR PTSD[Title/Abstract]
  OR "complex PTSD"[Title/Abstract]
  OR CPTSD[Title/Abstract]
  OR dissociation[Title/Abstract]
  OR dissociative[Title/Abstract]
)
AND
(
  "journal name here"[Journal]
)
```
